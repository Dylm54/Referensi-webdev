Work Programs

IEEE Education Movement

Objectives. Educational facilities for the participants by introducing latest technologies.
Advantages. Students of elementary school and/or high school learn subjects about the latest technology
Target. Students of elementary school and/or high school.
Output. Participants obtain information and insights regarding the presented subject.

PPK Ormawa

Objectives. Improve the quality of students in tertiary institutions.
Advantages. Enhance students' imagination concerning recent issues, in the form of change concept and/or development from various aspects of the nation, which are futuristic, long-term in nature, and have the potential to be realized.
Target. Students in general
Output. Sharpen students' hard and soft skills.

IEEE Webinar I

Objectives. Discuss and exchange ideas regarding a scientific problem.
Advantages. Hone soft skill and hard skill.
Target. Public and IEEE internals
Output. Knowledge and creativity.

IEEE Forum Discussion

Objectives. Means for learning, discussing, and honing critical thinking skills for each participant in accordance with the discussed subject matter.
Advantages. Improving human resources (SDM) for participants and committee members who attended the event. The subject matter discussed will help gain knowledge and new insights.
Target. Public/General audience and civitas akademika Telkom University
Output. Build up participants and committees' character along with carry out IEEE SB Telkom University social branding
